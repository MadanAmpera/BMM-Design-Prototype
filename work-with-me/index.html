a

The environment is the intervention

Most organisations try to change the outcome — the disengagement, the conflict, the missed targets. We work on the two things you can actually control: the conditions your people stand in, and how long they stand in them.

The core conviction

Neither the water nor the ice chose to change.

They responded to the environment they were placed in — and stayed in it long enough. Your people are no different. They are not resistant to change; they are responding to the environment they are currently in.

Change the environment. Sustain it. The transformation follows.

Variable 01

The right environment

Conducive conditions for the change you actually want — structures, norms, and leadership behaviours that make honesty safe.

Variable 02

The right duration

Held long enough for the new pattern to become the normal one. We mistake early difficulty for failure; time, in the right conditions, is not the enemy.

b
