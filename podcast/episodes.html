Memoirs to Millions × Breaking Mental Models

Ice, water, and the environments that shape us. 22 years of military structure meets a lifetime of crossing worlds — with guest Asher Wright, Army veteran, educator, and host of the Memoirs to Millions podcast.

▶ Listen now Recorded 2 Apr 2026 · 4:30pm ET

The guest

Asher Wright — Army veteran, educator, podcast host. A man who knows what it means when the structure that defined you suddenly disappears.

The setup

Two men who left home at 18, arriving at the same conclusion: the environment shapes the person — not the other way around.

What we explored

Ice & water. What a gap year needs to be to shift the environment, not pause it. Awareness as the elixir of life.

Questions that landed

Front stage vs backstage. Who are you being a hero to — right now? The version that stayed vs the one built for others.
