Chapter V · § ii

The seat is
open. Come
sit in it.

No requirement for a blue tick or a bestselling book. The only qualification is that you've lived something, learned something, or questioned something — and you're willing to say it out loud without the safety net of a rehearsed answer.

a

How it works

01

Reach out

Drop a message sharing what's on your mind. No formal pitch required — just tell us what you'd want to talk about and why it matters to you right now.

02

Pre-chat

A brief, informal conversation to get a feel for the territory. No prep needed — in fact, the less polished, the better.

03

Show up & talk

We record. No rigid agenda — three loose anchors and a real conversation. You don't need all the answers; you just need to be willing to be honest.

b

The elephant list

The conversations this show was built for — the ones that keep getting postponed, politely sidestepped, or dressed up in language that softens the edges too much. A starting point, not a limit.

01

Parenting & pressure

Are we raising children or managing our own anxiety? What we never say about how hard it actually is.

02

Success & emptiness

You did everything right — the job, the house, the title. So why does something still feel hollow?

03

Belonging vs assimilation

How much of yourself do you quietly edit to fit in — and what does that cost you over time?

04

Grief that has no name

The losses with no funeral. A version of yourself. A future you'd planned. A relationship that ended without closure.

c

Pitch your conversation

Form is a static mock-up — to be connected to the client's inbox / booking system on build.

The less polished, the better. We read every message.

The one rule

Be honest or don't bother.

This podcast is not inclusive — it's exclusive to all. Everyone's welcome, but comfort zones are not.
